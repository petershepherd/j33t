BUNDLED SNIPING AT LAUNCH
The creator buys from multiple wallets simultaneously at launch (bundle tx), securing 20–90% of supply at the lowest price. Artificial pump follows, FOMO kicks in, you buy high — they sell. Use RugCheck.xyz or Flintr to detect bundles. Avoid the first 1–2 minutes after launch.

WASH TRADING + VOLUME BOTS
The same wallets trade back and forth in tiny amounts (e.g. 0.01 SOL) to generate fake volume. The chart looks active, "huge interest" — everyone piles in. Check Dexscreener and Solscan: repeated txs between the same wallets, identical trade sizes, all new wallets = red flag.

DEV WALLET > 5%
If the dev controls more than 5% of supply at launch, that's a dump waiting to happen. Check top holders on Birdeye or Solscan — if concentration is high or multiple wallets show the same transaction patterns, walk away.

MINT & FREEZE AUTHORITY ACTIVE
